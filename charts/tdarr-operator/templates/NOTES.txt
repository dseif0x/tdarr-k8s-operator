How it works:
  The operator polls the server every {{ .Values.controller.pollInterval }}. When the
  transcode or health-check queue is non-empty it creates the node Job (with your
  configured runtimeClass/tolerations/resources). Once the queue drains and the
  node is idle for {{ .Values.controller.idleTimeout }}, the Job is deleted.
